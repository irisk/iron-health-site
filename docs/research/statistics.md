## Symptoms Listed

All symptoms listed on the site are from the Iron Health knowledge base (`iron-health/docs/knowledge.md`):
- Fatigue and reduced energy
- Brain fog, poor concentration
- Hair loss, brittle nails
- Cold intolerance
- Restless legs syndrome
- Impaired immune function
- Poor exercise recovery
- Elevated heart rate during exercise

These are well-documented symptoms of iron deficiency, supported by multiple clinical sources.
